Common Questions (Prashn)

What is Vedic Astrology (Jyotish)?

Jyotish, or Vedic Astrology, is the ancient Indian science of light that studies the movement and positions of celestial bodies and their influence on human life and natural events. It's based on the sidereal zodiac and uses specific calculations like Dasha systems.

How accurate is the birth time needed?

The exact birth time is crucial, especially for determining the Ascendant (Lagna) and the house placements, which significantly impact predictions. An error of even a few minutes can change important details. Please provide the time from your birth certificate if possible. If unsure, mention it during booking; birth time rectification might be possible as a separate service.

Are remedies guaranteed to work?

Remedies (Upayas) are suggested based on astrological principles to help mitigate negative influences or enhance positive ones. Their effectiveness depends on various factors, including the individual's karma, faith, and correct performance of the remedy. While they are intended to help, specific results cannot be guaranteed (as mentioned in our disclaimer).

What is the difference between Sun Sign and Moon Sign (Rashi)?

Western astrology primarily uses your Sun Sign (based on birth date). Vedic Astrology gives significant importance to your Moon Sign or Rashi (the zodiac sign where the Moon was placed at your birth time). Rashi is considered more indicative of your inner nature, emotions, and how you experience life. Our daily horoscopes are based on Rashi.
